About the Project

Welcome to the future of farming – the Smart Farm powered by IoT. This innovative agricultural system represents a revolution in the way we cultivate crops and manage livestock, offering a holistic approach to agriculture that integrates technology and data-driven insights.

Key features

Real-time Monitoring: Continuous monitoring of environmental conditions such as temperature, humidity, soil moisture, and light levels. Automated Irrigation: Precise control of irrigation systems based on soil conditions, weather forecasts, and crop needs. Crop Health Management: Early detection of diseases, pests, and nutrient deficiencies through sensors and cameras.
